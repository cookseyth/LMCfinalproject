Step-by-Step Guide to Using ChatGPT

Your manager has provided some day-to-day examples for your job. Here you can see how much more efficient you can be when you use ChatGPT in your daily tasks.

Exercise 1: Drafting a Simple Email (Easy)

Objective: Use ChatGPT to write a short email for scheduling a meeting.

  1. Open ChatGPT: Go to the ChatGPT interface.
  2. Prompt: Paste the following prompt: "Draft an email to schedule a meeting with the team on Monday at 10 AM."
  3. Review: Review the auto-generated email for accuracy and completeness.
  4. Edit: Make any necessary edits to fit your style or specific requirements.
  5. Send: Use the generated email to send to your team.

Example Output:

Subject: Meeting Scheduled for Monday at 10 AM

Dear Team,

I hope this email finds you well. I would like to schedule a meeting with everyone on Monday at 10 AM to discuss our upcoming project. Please confirm your availability.

Best regards,
[Your Name]



Exercise 2: Summarizing a Report (Medium)

Objective: Use ChatGPT to summarize a long report into key points.

  1. Download Example Report. You'll notice this example report is relatively long!
  2. Open ChatGPT: Go to the ChatGPT interface.
  3. Prompt: Paste the following prompt: "Summarize the attached report, highlighting important metrics with bullet points."
  4. Review: Have a look at the generated summary and make sure it is correct and complete; AI is not perfect.
  5. Edit: Make all relevant modifications if some crucial points are missing from the summary.
  6. Use: Finally, you can use the summarized points in your presentation or briefing.

Exercise 3: Combining & Analyzing Data (Advanced)

Objective: Use ChatGPT to analyze data, combine files, and give a report.

  1. Download data example Excel file 1.
  2. Download data example Excel file 2
  3. Open ChatGPT: Go to the ChatGPT interface.
  4. Prompt: Paste the following prompt: "Combine these files and add a column that tells what region each sale is from. Give me a report on the regional performance across regions."
  5. Review: Verify if the report is correct and complete. You can also have ChatGPT let you download the combined files!
